About the Owner

Abdul - Ganiyu Vinod Obasa is a dynamic entrepreneur with operations spanning various sectors in Nigeria. He holds a bachelor's degree in Accounting and Finance from University of Hertfordshire, England and a master's degree in Accounting and Finance from Aston University, Birmingham, England. He is currently pursuing his doctoral degree in Accounting ( Taxation & Fiscal Policy) at Babcock University, Ilisan Remo, Ogun State, Nigeria. Beyond his professional endeavors, Abdul - Ganiyu is deeply committed to Sport, community development and volunteerism.
